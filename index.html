Eriksholm

Level Design (Unreal Engine 5)


When Hanna’s brother, Herman, disappears and the police begin to pursue him, her search sets off a chain of catastrophic events that will not only alter their lives but also the destiny of the entire city.

Eriksholm is a single-player, story-driven, stealth/puzzle game developed by Gothenburg-based River End Games.

My contributions to Eriksholm:
  • designed puzzles / player encounters
  • implemented level structure
  • blocked out levels
  • scripted NPC behavior and events
  • mocked up and polished in-game cutscenes
  • implemented VO and animations
  • facilitated playtests and followed up on player feedback

ReLeap

Project Management and Sound Design (Unity)


ReLeap is a single player, 2D side scroller runner. A five minute experience where you jump between rooftops, dodge obstacles, and angle your charge jumps to make the perfect landings. The game is played on a custom-built arcade-like controller which consists of a handlebar that you can push and pull to jump and charge your jumps as well as a foot slider to slide beneath obstacles.

My contributions to ReLeap:
  • managed project deadlines and workflows
  • facilitated group discussions and meetings
  • organized, facilitated, and documented all playtest sessions
  • developed a business strategy and business model
  • secured funding for my team to travel to GDC
  • pitched ReLeap to investors in San Francisco at GDC
  • produced all game music and SFX in Ableton Live and implemented music transitions in FMOD and Unity
  • worked on level design in Unity

ReLeap was a project developed in 10 weeks during my game education where my main roles were scrum master and sound designer.

We've showcased the game and booth at:

  • Gotland Game Conference 2018
  • Swedish Game Awards 2018
  • Game Developers Conference 2019 (alternative control exhibit)

During GDC 2019 we also pitched a mobile prototype of ReLeap at the Silicon Viking Investor and Big Indie Pitch events.













Provincia

Project Management and Sound Design (Unity)


Provincia is an action-adventure dogfighting game. The player plays as a bounty hunter piloting their own personalized aircraft through a wide open world, collecting quests and favors as well as meeting different colorful characters along the way.

My contributions to Provincia:

  • managed project deadlines and workflows
  • facilitated group discussions and meetings
  • organized, facilitated, and documented all playtest sessions
  • produced all SFX in Ableton Live and implemented them in FMOD
  • created and implemented an interactive music system in FMOD
  • pitched Provincia at the Gotland Game Conference 2019

The game was part of a 10 week vertical slice project in my game education where my main roles were scrum master and sound designer.

Provincia was showcased at the:

  • Gotland Game Conference 2019
  • Swedish Game Awards 2019
